Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Alert Bridge (“Alert Bridge”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our email-to-SMS alerting service (the “Service”).

Alert Bridge is designed to help organizations convert specific inbound emails into SMS notifications for their authorized recipients. We process certain personal data on behalf of our customers so they can deliver time-sensitive alerts to their teams.

1. Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to:

  • Customer administrators and users who configure and manage Alert Bridge.
  • Recipients who receive SMS messages sent through Alert Bridge on behalf of a customer.
  • Visitors to our informational website at alertbridge.app.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information provided by customers

  • Account and contact details such as name, email address, organization, and role.
  • Configuration data such as routing rules, group names, and associated email addresses used for alerts.
  • Recipient data such as mobile phone numbers, display names, and optional metadata needed to deliver notifications.

2.2 Information processed as part of alerts

When our Service is used as designed, we process parts of email and SMS content to deliver alerts and maintain an audit trail:

  • Inbound email details such as sender address, subject, body text (in plain text form), and message identifiers.
  • Outbound SMS details such as the message text, recipient phone numbers, delivery status, and optional error codes from SMS providers.
  • Log metadata such as timestamps, routing decisions, and correlation IDs used to connect inbound messages with resulting SMS notifications.

2.3 Website and service usage data

We may collect limited technical information about visits to alertbridge.app or our administrative tools, such as:

  • IP address, browser type, and basic device information.
  • Pages visited and actions taken within the admin interface, to help us troubleshoot and improve the Service.

3. How we use information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and operate the Service, including converting emails into SMS alerts and logging those events.
  • Authenticate and authorize customer administrators and users.
  • Maintain the security, reliability, and integrity of the Service, including monitoring for abuse.
  • Respond to customer support requests and technical inquiries.
  • Analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve performance and usability.
  • Comply with legal obligations and carrier requirements for messaging.

4. Legal basis for processing

Our legal basis for processing personal data depends on the context in which it is collected and used. In most cases, we process data:

  • To perform a contract with our customers (for example, operating the Service as described in our agreements).
  • For our legitimate interests in providing a secure and reliable alerting service, where those interests are not overridden by the rights and interests of data subjects.
  • To comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements, such as telecom and messaging rules.

5. How we share information

We do not sell personal data. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With SMS and communications providers in order to deliver text messages to recipients’ devices.
  • With infrastructure and service providers that help us operate the Service (for example, hosting, logging, and email services), under appropriate data protection agreements.
  • With customer organizations that control the accounts and phone numbers configured in Alert Bridge, as part of logs, reporting, or account management.
  • When required by law or to protect rights, safety, or security, in response to valid legal requests or to enforce our Terms and Conditions.

6. Data retention

We retain message logs and associated content for periods that support our customers’ operational and compliance needs. Retention periods may vary by subscription plan or customer configuration.

When data is no longer needed for the purposes described in this policy, we will take reasonable steps to delete or irreversibly anonymize it.

7. Your choices and rights

7.1 Recipients of SMS alerts

If you receive SMS alerts via Alert Bridge, your relationship is primarily with the organization that configured the Service (for example, your employer or service provider). That organization determines which phone numbers are used and what alerts are sent.

Where applicable, SMS messages will include information about how to opt out of further alerts (for example, replying with a specific keyword). You can also contact the sending organization directly if you have questions about why you are receiving alerts.

7.2 Rights under data protection laws

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal data. For data processed on behalf of a customer, we may redirect your request to the relevant customer organization, which acts as the controller of that data.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we process, including access controls, encryption in transit where appropriate, and monitoring for unusual activity.

No system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. We encourage customers to follow best practices for account security, including strong authentication and role-based access to administrative tools.

9. International transfers

Alert Bridge may use infrastructure or service providers located in different countries. Where personal data is transferred across borders, we will take appropriate steps to protect it in accordance with applicable law and this policy.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our Service, legal requirements, or business practices. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date above. If changes are material, we may provide additional notice through the Service or via email to customer contacts.
