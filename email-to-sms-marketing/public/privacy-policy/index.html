Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Alert Bridge, operated by D L Brandt LLC ("we", "us", or "our"), collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the Alert Bridge email-to-SMS alert platform ("Alert Bridge", the "Service").

Overview

Alert Bridge is an enterprise notification service used by organizations to deliver operational and facility-related alerts to designated employees, contractors, and on-call personnel via SMS. The Service is intended solely for internal operational use by customer organizations and is not marketed to individual consumers.

Information We Process

We process the following categories of information:

  • Business contact information such as name, work email address, and mobile phone number
  • Tenant and configuration data provided by administrators
  • Inbound email content stored in logs, which may include message subject, body, headers, routing details, timestamps, and delivery outcomes. If enabled, logs may also include attachments or attachment metadata.
  • Phone verification and consent events, such as one-time-code (OTP) send attempts, verification attempts, timestamps, and delivery outcomes.
  • Access and audit logs required to operate and secure the Service, which may include the phone number used for verification, tenant/organization context, timestamps, and technical request metadata (for example IP address and browser/device details) when collected.
  • System logs required to operate, secure, and audit the Service

Information We Do Not Collect

Alert Bridge is designed to process only operational alert information. We do not collect or store:

  • Personal SMS conversations or message threads
  • Payment information or financial account numbers
  • Government-issued identifiers (e.g., Social Security Numbers)
  • Biometric data or facial recognition information
  • Precise location data beyond what carriers provide
  • Protected health information (PHI), unless permitted under a separate compliance agreement

How Phone Numbers Are Obtained

Phone numbers are provided through an administrator-managed invitation workflow. Authorized tenant administrators invite recipients by sharing an enrollment link through their normal channels (for example email, chat, or ticketing), based on internal processes such as on-call schedules, facilities teams, or contractor lists.

Recipients enter their own mobile phone number and confirm control using phone verification (one-time code) before enabling SMS delivery. Administrators do not need to enter phone numbers into Alert Bridge to complete enrollment.

Use of SMS and Message Content

Phone numbers are used to deliver platform-branded operational notifications from Alert Bridge. Outbound SMS messages are a generic notification with a secure link. Detailed alert content (including the inbound email content that triggered the alert) is available in the Alert Bridge web log after phone verification.

Alert Bridge does not send marketing or promotional SMS messages.

Message and data rates may apply depending on the recipient’s mobile plan.

Recipient Opt-Out (STOP) and Re-Enable (START)

Recipients may reply STOP to any Alert Bridge SMS message to disable further SMS delivery. A confirmation message is sent after STOP is processed. If a recipient later wishes to resume notifications, they may reply START to re-enable SMS delivery, followed by a confirmation message.

If a recipient replies STOP, they will no longer receive operational notifications or phone verification codes from Alert Bridge until they reply START to re-enable SMS delivery.

STOP and START actions affect SMS delivery for the recipient’s phone number. Tenant administrators may still manage recipient records (for example, enrolling or removing recipients) according to internal processes, but SMS delivery remains disabled until the recipient re-enables it with START.

How We Use Information

The information we process is used to:

  • Deliver operational SMS notifications and verification codes to authorized recipients
  • Capture inbound email messages into logs for tenant-scoped alert history and troubleshooting
  • Route inbound email messages to the appropriate tenant for processing
  • Maintain audit logs for operational and security purposes
  • Provide administrative tools for managing enrollment, recipients, and routing rules
  • Monitor system performance, delivery outcomes, and platform reliability
  • Prevent abuse and investigate suspicious activity

Legal Basis for Processing

Alert Bridge processes information under the direction of customer organizations and based on their legitimate business interests in delivering operational alerts to authorized personnel. Processing may also occur as necessary to perform the Service, maintain security, or comply with legal obligations.

Processing on Behalf of Customer Organizations

Alert Bridge processes information under the instructions of the customer organization administering the tenant. Customer organizations retain ownership of all data they provide or generate. We do not access customer data except as required to operate, support, troubleshoot, or secure the Service.

Data Retention

Alert Bridge retains operational logs—including inbound email content stored in logs, routing metadata, delivery status, audit trails, and timestamps—based on the retention period of the customer’s subscription plan:

  • Starter Plan: 30 days
  • Standard Plan: 90 days
  • Professional Plan: 180 days
  • Enterprise Plan: 365 days

Logs older than the retention period are permanently deleted and cannot be recovered. Customer organizations may request earlier deletion, subject to operational or security requirements.

Information Sharing

We share information only with service providers necessary to deliver the Service, such as telecommunications carriers and SMS routing providers. We do not sell personal information or share it for independent commercial purposes.

Data Storage and Transfers

Information processed by Alert Bridge is stored and transmitted using Microsoft Azure infrastructure in the United States, primarily in the West US 2 region. All data transfers occur using encrypted communication channels.

Access to Information

Access to customer data is restricted to authorized personnel at D L Brandt LLC who require such access to operate, support, or secure the Service. Access is limited, logged where appropriate, and used only for operational purposes such as troubleshooting, maintenance, or investigating abuse.

Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information processed by Alert Bridge. No system can be guaranteed completely secure, and we disclaim absolute security of stored or transmitted information.

Your Choices

If you receive Alert Bridge SMS notifications and wish to stop receiving them, you may reply STOP at any time. To resume notifications, reply START. If you have opted out by replying STOP, you will not receive operational notifications or phone verification codes until you reply START.

You may also request changes through your organization’s designated administrator.

Customer Responsibilities

Customer organizations are responsible for ensuring that information they provide is accurate and authorized, securing administrative access to their tenant, and responding to internal requests regarding enrollment or removal. Customer misconfiguration or unauthorized access is outside the control of Alert Bridge.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes occur, we will update the effective date and notify customer organizations when appropriate.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how Alert Bridge handles data, contact us at support@alertbridge.app.
