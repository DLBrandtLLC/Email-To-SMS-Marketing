What happens after you subscribe

Subscribing creates a private tenant for your organization. It does not automatically start texting anyone. SMS delivery begins only after you finish setup, connect your alerting systems, and invite recipients.

For internal operational alerts only — not for marketing or promotional messaging.

Simple steps

1) Subscribe

Complete checkout using our hosted billing provider (Lemon Squeezy). This starts automated onboarding for your organization.

2) Claim your tenant and sign in

You receive a “Finish Setup” email link. Open it to claim your tenant. Then sign in with Microsoft to become the first Tenant Administrator.

3) Add a route, groups, and invite recipients

Create a route (an inbound email address) and define the groups that should be notified. Then invite recipients by sending them an enrollment link through your normal channels (email, chat, ticketing, etc.).

Recipients follow the link and opt in by verifying their phone number with a one-time code.

4) Connect your systems and start sending alerts

Tell your teams (on-call, facilities, property operations, and IT/OT) where to send alert emails — your new Alert Bridge inbound email address. Once your systems start emailing that address, Alert Bridge captures each inbound alert into logs and sends periodic notifications with a secure link. Use Logs to confirm receipt and review details.

Recipients do not need accounts; they verify phone control to view logs.

Good to know

  • Invitation-based enrollment: administrators invite recipients by sending an enrollment link. Recipients opt in after phone verification.
  • Operational only: Alert Bridge does not send marketing or promotional messages.
  • Recipient control: recipients may reply STOP to disable delivery and START to resume, or use the SMS Consent page. If a recipient replies STOP, they will not receive operational notifications or phone verification codes until they reply START.

See SMS Program for the public program description and opt-in/opt-out details.

What if I want to change plans later?

Plan changes do not create a new tenant. Your tenant remains persistent for the life of your customer relationship. Upgrades apply immediately. Downgrades use a “grandfather + block growth” model: your existing configuration keeps working, but new creation is blocked until you are back within the new limits.
