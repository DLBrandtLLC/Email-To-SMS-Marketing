What Alert Bridge does

Email in, SMS out

Point alerting systems at a dedicated email address. Alert Bridge receives the message, stores it in your alert log, and sends a generic notification and secure link to view details.

Route by address and group

Use intuitive routes to map inbound email addresses to on-call groups, campuses, or escalation paths. You stay in control of who gets notified and when.

Designed for operations work

Alert Bridge is built for teams that already rely on email-based alerts but need a straightforward bridge into SMS — without replacing existing systems or re-architecting critical workflows.

How it works

Think of Alert Bridge like a secure “heads-up” for important alerts. Your systems send the technical details by email, and Alert Bridge sends a short text to let the right people know something needs attention.

  1. Your system emails an alert to Alert Bridge.
  2. Alert Bridge saves it in your secure alert log.
  3. Alert Bridge sends a short text (for example, no more than one message every 10 minutes when new alerts are present) with a link.
  4. You tap the link and verify your phone so we know it’s really you.
  5. You see the full details in the alert log.

SMS stays simple and consistent. The detailed alert content stays behind the secure link.
