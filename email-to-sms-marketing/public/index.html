What Alert Bridge does

Email in, SMS out

Point alerting systems at a dedicated email address. Alert Bridge receives the message, applies your routing rules, and delivers compact SMS notifications to the right recipients or groups.

Route by address and group

Use intuitive routes to map inbound email addresses to on-call groups, campuses, or escalation paths. You stay in control of who gets notified and when.

Designed for operations work

Alert Bridge is built for teams that already rely on email-based alerts but need a straightforward bridge into SMS — without replacing existing systems or re-architecting critical workflows.

Respectful messaging

We take consent, data protection, and message reliability seriously. Our goal is to help organizations reach people when it matters, without spamming or surprise charges.

  • Clear opt-out instructions in every SMS alert where applicable.
  • No sale of personal data to third parties.
  • Log retention and access aligned to your organization’s policies.
