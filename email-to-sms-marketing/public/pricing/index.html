Upgrades, downgrades, and enforcement

Alert Bridge uses a single-tenant continuity model: your tenant and configuration remain intact when you change plans. Plan changes update limits for the existing tenant (no tenant recreation, no reconfiguration).

Upgrades

Upgrades apply immediately. Existing routes, groups, and recipients continue to function, and the higher limits allow new creation right away.

Downgrades

Downgrades follow a “grandfather + block growth” model: if your tenant exceeds the new plan limits, existing configuration continues to operate, but creation of new routes/groups/recipients is blocked until you return within limits. Editing and deletion remain available so you can reduce usage without contacting support.

Usage thresholds

The admin console surfaces warnings as usage approaches monthly limits. When limits are reached, blocked messages are logged and visible in Message Logs.

SMS program & consent

Alert Bridge is an administrator-managed operational messaging program. Recipients are enrolled by customer administrators based on an existing business relationship and operational role. Recipients may reply STOP to disable delivery and START to resume.

See SMS Program for full program details and SMS Consent to manage opt-in/opt-out.
