Alert Bridge SMS Program Details

Alert Bridge is an enterprise notification platform used by organizations to deliver operational and facility-related alerts to designated employees, contractors, and on-call staff via SMS. Alerts are generated by business systems such as building management systems, monitoring tools, and IT alerting systems.

  • Program type: Operational alerts only (no marketing or promotional messages)
  • Recurring program: Recurring operational notifications may be sent. Frequency varies based on events (no fixed schedule).
  • Fees: Message and data rates may apply.

Who Receives SMS Alerts

SMS alerts are sent only to individuals who have an existing business relationship with a subscribing organization. Typical recipients include:

  • Employees responsible for facilities, operations, or IT services
  • Contractors and vendors engaged by the organization
  • On-call staff designated by the organization

How Recipients Join an Organization’s Alert List (Opt-In)

Alert Bridge requires consent before sending SMS messages to a phone number. Organizations invite recipients using a secure link, and recipients confirm participation by verifying their phone number.

1) Administrator Sends an Invitation Link

An authorized administrator in a subscribing organization selects the intended recipient and sends them a secure invitation (“magic link”). This invitation identifies the organization and is used to enroll the recipient into that organization’s alert list.

2) Recipient Verifies Their Phone Number and Consents

The recipient opens the link and verifies their phone with a one-time verification code. After verification, the recipient may enable SMS alerts. This ensures consent is unambiguous and that messages are sent only to the phone number provided by the recipient.

Recipients can manage their SMS delivery status at any time using our SMS Consent page.

Opt-Out and Re-Enable (STOP / START / Web)

Message recipients may revoke consent and opt out of receiving future messages at any time. Alert Bridge supports multiple opt-out options:

  • Reply STOP to any message sent from Alert Bridge.
  • Use the SMS Consent page (phone verification required) to opt out.
  • Contact support: support@alertbridge.app.

When a recipient opts out (for example via STOP), SMS delivery to that phone number is disabled globally for Alert Bridge until the recipient explicitly re-enables it.

To resume receiving operational alerts after opting out, the recipient may reply START or re-enable SMS delivery using the SMS Consent page (with phone verification).

HELP and Support

Recipients may reply HELP to receive support contact information and a link to the SMS Consent page, or email support@alertbridge.app.

Message Content

SMS messages sent by Alert Bridge are strictly operational in nature and may include:

  • Alarms or alerts from building management systems
  • Fault or status notifications for critical equipment
  • Operational notifications that require on-call or facilities response

Alert Bridge does not send marketing or promotional SMS messages.
