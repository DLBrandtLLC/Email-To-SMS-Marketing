Service Description

Alert Bridge enables organizations to convert inbound operational alert emails into platform-branded SMS notifications that include a secure link to view alert details in a web log. Outbound SMS messages are a generic notification and secure link; detailed alert content is displayed on the web after phone verification. The Service is intended for internal operational use and is not designed for marketing, consumer engagement, entertainment, or general communication.

Secure Link Access and Phone Verification

Alert Bridge uses a secure link + phone verification model to protect alert details and reduce risk of sensitive or inappropriate content being delivered over SMS.

  • Secure link: SMS notifications include a secure link to view alert details in the Alert Bridge web log.
  • Phone verification: Recipients must verify control of their phone number using a one-time code before viewing alert details.
  • Expiration and anti-forwarding intent: Links, sessions, and verification tokens are time-limited and are designed to reduce the usefulness of forwarded messages. Recipients may be asked to verify again after a period of time, or if risk signals are detected.
  • Tenant-scoped access: After verification, recipients can only view alerts for organizations/tenants they are currently authorized to access.

Recipient Enrollment, Delivery Status, and Opt-Out

Recipients are enrolled through an administrator-managed invitation workflow based on an existing business relationship and operational need. Administrators invite recipients by sending an enrollment link through their normal channels. Recipients enter their phone number and confirm control using phone verification before enabling SMS delivery.

Recipients may reply STOP to any Alert Bridge SMS message to disable further SMS delivery. A confirmation message is sent after STOP is processed. Recipients may reply START to resume receiving notifications.

Strict STOP behavior: If a recipient replies STOP, they will no longer receive operational notifications or phone verification codes from Alert Bridge until they reply START.

Administrator override: Administrators cannot override a recipient’s STOP choice to force SMS delivery. Administrators may manage enrollment eligibility and tenant configuration, but SMS delivery remains disabled until the recipient re-enables it with START.
